Void is a privacy-first encrypted messenger.

No email. No phone number. No cloud accounts. Your identity is created on your device and lives there.

Features:
- End-to-end encrypted messaging using ECDH + AES-256-GCM
- Self-destructing messages that vanish after being read
- Self-destructing notes that can be read once then are destroyed
- On-device identity using BIP39 recovery phrases
- Transfer your identity with a 6-digit code
- No signup required

Void stores only encrypted data on its servers. Nobody, not even the developer, can read your messages.

Open source. Free forever. No ads. No tracking.
